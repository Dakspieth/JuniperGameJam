My friend and I started this project on the third day of the seven-day jam, so we were really strapped for time, but it is what it is.

This game follows John, who comes into work and needs to hand in a (serious) paper before the deadline. John needs to find and retrieve the paper, then go all the way up to his boss' office in time. If you don't get the paper to the boss in time or you break a rule, you restart, spinning a wheel that adds another rule or modifier to your game, either changing the way you play.
Some examples of the "rules" are:
- Red light green light: a stoplight pops onto the GUI and periodically turns red, and if you walk during this time you restart
- Sugar high: you move faster than normal for the first 30 seconds of the round, then you move slower than normal
- Max jumps: If you jump more than 75 times in a round, you restart
The controls for the game are as follows:
- Walk left/right: Arrows or A/D
- Jump: Up arrow (may or may not have forgotten to add "W" as a jump key)
- Sprint: Shift (Sprint only applies if you have the modifier that lets you sprint)
- Pause: Escape
